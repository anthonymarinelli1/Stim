Founder & CEO · San Diego, CA
The Origin

It started with a question.

As a college senior, Chase Basson enjoyed beer like most people his age. But when he started paying attention to what he was eating and drinking, he was surprised by what he found — or rather, what he couldn't find. Clean-label organic options in the beer aisle were basically nonexistent, especially in the light, easy-drinking lager styles that most people actually reach for.

"I care about what goes into my body. I was shocked by how few organic, clean-label options exist in beer. So I decided to build one."

The gap nobody was filling.

Organic food had taken over grocery stores. Organic skincare was mainstream. But beer? The category had barely moved. Studies were finding glyphosate residue in popular brands, consumers were asking questions, and nobody was answering them with an affordable, great-tasting alternative.

Chase saw that as an opportunity. STIM — short for Stimulate — was his answer.

Built lean, built smart.

Rather than building a brewery from scratch, Chase partnered with Protector Brewery in San Diego — a certified organic, veteran-owned operation with an experienced production team. This lets STIM focus entirely on what matters most: the brand, the product, and getting it into people's hands.

Contract brewed with certified organic partners
Every batch third-party tested for glyphosate residue
Priced to compete with mainstream — not just health food stores
Starting local in San Diego, proving demand before scaling
The Vision

Organic beer for everyone.

STIM isn't trying to be the hippest craft IPA or the most premium import. It's trying to be the beer you reach for on a Tuesday — except one you actually feel good about drinking. Clean ingredients, honest label, great taste. That's it.
